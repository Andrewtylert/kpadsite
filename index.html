About us

Kansas Progress & Advancement Defense (KPAD): Our mission is to spotlight the disconnect between voters and lawmakers, defend the rights and futures of everyday Kansans, and drive long-overdue change through education, advocacy, and action.Year after year, those in power block progress—not because the people dont want it, but because they dont care to hear it. Were here to change that.

My Name: Andrew Thompson


Welcome to the fight. This isn’t about left vs. right—it’s about representation vs. obstruction. We’re building a movement to defend the future Kansans actually want. No more excuses. No more silence.

Please use the contact section at the bottom of the page if you have any interest in joining our organization or would like to offer any support.

We are a growing grassroots movement created and led by everyday Kansans who are tired of being ignored. We’re young, we’re energized, and done waiting for progress that 60–70% of the state already supports. We believe in expanding healthcare, legalizing cannabis, protecting basic civil rights, and investing in a clean future that works for everyone—not just the loudest lawmakers in Topeka.

(KPAD) is here to inform and build power from the ground up.
